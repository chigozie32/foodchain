Delete Your FoodChain Account

You can permanently delete your FoodChain account directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the FoodChain App.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮).
  4. Open Profile.
  5. Tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm the deletion request.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently removed.
  • Your personal information will be deleted.
  • You will no longer be able to access your account.
  • Certain records such as completed orders may be retained only where required by law or for fraud prevention.

Need Help?

Contact us at services@yamehsystems.com.ng if you experience any issues deleting your account.
