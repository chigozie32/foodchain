Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At FoodChain, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and manage your personal information when you use our website and mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery addresses
  • Order information
  • Restaurant and rider registration details

How We Use Your Information

  • Process food orders.
  • Connect customers with restaurants and delivery riders.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve our services and user experience.
  • Send important service notifications.

Data Security

FoodChain uses secure technologies to protect your personal information. All sensitive information transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is only shared when necessary to complete your order or comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account and personal information at any time.
