FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need To Know

Here are answers to the questions businesses ask most before joining the FoodChain platform.

Is it free to register?

Yes. Registration is completely free. After your application has been reviewed, the FoodChain team will contact you if your business is approved.

Who can become a partner?

Restaurants, supermarkets, pharmacies, grocery stores, bakeries, and other approved food-related businesses are welcome to apply.

How long does approval take?

Most applications are reviewed within 24 to 72 hours. If additional information is required, a FoodChain representative will contact you directly.

How will I receive customer orders?

During our initial launch, FoodChain manages customer orders on behalf of our partners. When an order is assigned to your business, our team will contact you directly by phone or WhatsApp with the order details and delivery instructions.
